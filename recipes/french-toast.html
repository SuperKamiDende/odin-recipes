French Toast

Description

One of my favourite easy to make home dishes, French Toast has a unique tender, fluffy and very tasty element to it that ranks it one of my top recipes someone should learn.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk,eggs,vanilla,cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Now you are done! Serve hot and enjoy your meal.
